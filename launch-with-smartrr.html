Get started
on your timeline.

For the most basic subscriptions programs, Smartrr can be installed in under ten minutes. For more complex launches, our team will guide you through the entire process.

Step 1
Meet your dedicated implementation team

At kickoff, you’ll be paired with a dedicated Customer Success Manager (CSM) and Implementation Engineer. They’ll be with you every step of the process to ensure you’re set up for lasting success on Smartrr.

Step 2
Configure your subscriptions in Smartrr

You let us know exactly how you want to set up your subscription programs, and we’ll take care of the heavy lifting. This includes the subscription type, discounts, shipping frequencies, bundles, and the front end design of your subscription widget and customer portal.

Step 3
Test and validate your programs

After configuration is complete you'll have a chance to review and validate that everything is working properly in a test environment before anything is activated on our end.

Step 4
Launch Smartrr!

Once you are satisfied everything is ready to go, activate your subscription programs on Smartrr. You won’t be charged for anything up until this point and you will officially be live on Smartrr!
