Frequently Asked Questions

When does the 14-day free trial of Smartrr start?

Your 30-day free trial starts when you download the Smartrr app on Shopify. You will never be billed without your consent or until you select a paid plan in Shopify.

Will I ever be forced to upgrade my plan?

Never! We’ll never force you to upgrade your plan based on usage or features moving to a more expensive tier. You can also upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time by contacting our support team.

How is the 1% of subscription GMV calculated?

The 1% of GMV is calculated on all orders involving a subscription. This includes first orders that are a subscription and all subsequent reorders attached to the original subscription purchase.

How can I cancel my Smartrr plan?

You can cancel you Smartrr plan at any time by contacting our support team. We work on a monthly basis, so you will be billed through the end of the month in which you cancel your plan.
